Paddy harvester

paddy harvester is a self-propelled petrol-operated walk-behind type harvester with rotary blades which helps to cut crops effectively, conveys it vertically to one end and windrows the crops on the ground uniformly without any shattering of the crop. Collection of crops for making bundles is easy and it is done manually. In addition, the machine is equipped with tires with a wider diameter, so it can be widely applicable in the plain, mountain, hilly areas, upland & slopping field

peanut harvester

Peanut harvesting equipment is mainly used to harvest peanuts from the field, and Taizy two-row peanut harvesting equipment can be operated flexibly in the field with a 20-35 hp tractor. And its working efficiency is high, which can reach 1300-200m2 per hour. What’s more, this peanut harvester does not break peanuts, its breakage rate is less than 1%.
